Things to Do

Pittsford Village / Canal

Some of our favorite things to do here include:

  • Grabbing a coffee and breakfast from Village Bakery and going for a walk or run along the canal
  • Hot yoga at Breathe with smoothies from their Juice Bar afterwards
  • Having a beer/cider at Lock 32, sitting outside and listening to live music
  • Garbage plates from Hungry’s and beers at Thirsty’s (the local watering hole)
  • Obligatory stops for ice cream at Pittsford Dairy

Park Avenue

Maddie lived here her senior year of college! Park Ave is a trendy neighborhood downtown with lots of fun restaurants and cocktail bars. Our favorites are Verns for Italian food and Dorado for Mexican food!
