FAQs

What is the dress code?

Formal evening attire. Per club policy, please refrain from wearing denim. We can’t wait to see our favorite people dressed up for the occasion!

Can I bring a plus one?

We are looking forward to celebrating our marriage with our closest friends and family. That being said, we are only able to accommodate those who are listed on the formal invitation. Thank you for understanding!

What time should I arrive at the ceremony?

Please arrive at least 15 minutes early to the Nazareth University Linehan Chapel. We’d appreciate a prompt start to save time on the dance floor!

Is parking available at the ceremony and reception?

Yes and yes! At Nazareth, parking is available in the lot adjacent to the Linehan Chapel (Lot Q) and the lot in front of the building (Lot R). Parking on the circular sidewalk in front of the building is not allowed. Enter the chapel from the front of the building (in front of Lot R).

Ample parking is available at Oak Hill Country Club, and complimentary valet will be provided for all guests. Cars can be left here overnight.

Who painted your Save the Dates?!

Maddie’s dear friend, Hannah Gilges painted the Save the Date, crest, and some other fun images you’ll see at the wedding! Hannah has painted many gifts for Maddie’s friends and family over the years. If you’re interested in made to order art, you can email her at gilgeshannah@gmail.com.
